Grade 1

  • Unassisted floating (front and back)
  • Push and glide from wall
  • Basic leg actions (kick on front and back with float)
  • Monday 4-5 pm - (Coach James)

Grade 2

  • Travel a short distance (e.g. 5–10m) with a recognizable stroke
  • Improved breathing technique (blowing bubbles, side breathing intro)

  • Wednesday 4-5 pm - (Coach Albert)

Grade 3

  • Swim at least 10–15m on front and back
  • Begin learning breaststroke and butterfly kick
  • Introduction to treading water
  • Monday 6-7 pm - (Coach Albert)
  • Friday 4-5 pm - (Coach Olivia)

Grade 4

  • Swim at least 20–25m confidently in 2–3 different strokes
  • Demonstrate basic survival skills (e.g., floating for 30 seconds)
  • Show correct breathing techniques
  • Monday 5-6 pm - (Coach Ren)
  • Wednesday 6-7 pm - (Coach James)

Grade 5 (Intermediate)

  • Swim at least 50m in 3–4 strokes with good technique
  • Demonstrate safe entries (step-in, jump, possibly shallow dive if age-appropriate)
  • Perform survival skills (tread water, float, roll to breathe)
  • Show awareness of water safety rules
  • Wednesday 5-6 pm - (Coach James)
